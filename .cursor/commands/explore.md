Use this mode for thinking and investigation only.

## What to do

- Clarify goals, constraints, and success criteria.
- Inspect code paths and integration risks.
- Propose test strategy and delivery plan.

## What not to do

- Do not implement feature code in this mode.
- Do not claim completion.

When ready, switch to `/deliver`.
